# Version 2.3 — Full Minor Penalty On Times

Penalty cards now show a Back On Ice time for every standard two-minute minor.

- Ordinary minor: Back On Ice is exactly two minutes of game-clock time after Off Ice.
- Power-play goal: the selected penalty's Back On Ice time is overridden with the goal time.
- Penalties carrying into the next period show the exact next-period clock time.
- Review-only penalty situations remain flagged for manual confirmation.

This supplies GameSheet with complete penalty intervals so it can automatically recognize shorthanded goals.
