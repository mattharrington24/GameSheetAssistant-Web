# GameSheet Assistant Version 2 Workflow

## Changes

- Workflow is organized by period.
- Goals and penalties are mixed chronologically within each period.
- Shots are the final card for each period.
- Starting-goalie cards appear immediately after game information.
- Team names are prominent and bold on event cards.
- Standard minor penalties affected by a power-play goal are highlighted with the required Back On Ice time.
- Ambiguous overlapping-minor situations are flagged for manual review.
- Final goalie review and final totals remain at the end.

## Important goalie note

When more than one goalie played for a team, SportsEngine final statistics do not reliably identify which goalie started. The workflow displays all goalies who played and asks the operator to confirm the starter.
